whole cane sugar

Cane sugar is extracted from sugarcane, then treated to remove impurities. The resulting juice is boiled, and as it cools the liquid crystallizes to produce sugar crystals. Whole cane sugar is not essential to good health. It is used in cooking, but also to produce ethanol fuel.

Weight: 1 cup | 7 3/4 oz | 220 g
